# Welcome to BlastCAD

[cite_start]**BlastCAD** is a high-performance, web-based 3D CAD platform specifically designed for underground blasting and mining engineering.  [cite_start]It combines traditional CAD drafting capabilities with advanced blasting logistics. 

### Core Capabilities
* [cite_start]**Precise 2D/3D Drafting:** Full suite of geometry tools with a command-line interface. 
* [cite_start]**Industry Integration:** Seamlessly import and export Surpac (.str), Micromine (.str), and AutoCAD (.dxf) files. 
* [cite_start]**Advanced Charging:** Design complex charging templates with real-time explosive density and primer calculations. 
* [cite_start]**Cloud Synchronized:** Access your projects from any desktop without installation. [cite: 2, 6]
