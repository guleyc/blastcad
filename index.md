# Welcome to BlastCAD

**BlastCAD** is a high-performance, web-based 3D CAD platform specifically designed for underground blasting and mining engineering.  It combines traditional CAD drafting capabilities with advanced blasting logistics. 

### Core Capabilities
* **Precise 2D/3D Drafting:** Full suite of geometry tools with a command-line interface. 
* **Industry Integration:** Seamlessly import and export Surpac (.str), Micromine (.str), and AutoCAD (.dxf) files. 
* **Advanced Charging:** Design complex charging templates with real-time explosive density and primer calculations. 
* **Cloud Synchronized:** Access your projects from any desktop without installation. [cite: 2, 6]
